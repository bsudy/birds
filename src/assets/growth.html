Growth &
Feeding Growth and feeding

Following the delicate hatch, the nest becomes a site of frantic energy. The Falco tinnunculus nestlings undergo a metamorphic transformation, their weight increasing nearly tenfold within the first fortnight.

"The primary parent maintains a constant vigil, shredding high-protein prey into manageable portions for the ever-gaping beaks."

As the soft down is replaced by sturdy juvenile plumage, the fledglings begin to exercise their pectoral muscles. They stand at the rim of the nest, peering into the abyss, instinctively sensing the call of the first flight.
